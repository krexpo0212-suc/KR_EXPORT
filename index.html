About Us

Who Is Rokis Enterprise?

Rokis Enterprise is a GST-registered Moringa export business, a GST-registered business based in Tamil Nadu, India. We specialize in exporting premium Moringa products to health-conscious markets in the UAE and Singapore.

We work directly with Moringa farmers across Tamil Nadu — ensuring fair pricing at the source and consistent, premium-grade product at the destination. Our goal: to make Indian Moringa the most trusted in Gulf and Southeast Asian markets.

Every shipment carries the full weight of Indian export compliance — GSTIN, PAN, phytosanitary certificates and lab analysis — so your import process is smooth and your buyers are confident.
